Why Periscope?

Perioperative assessment requires comprehensive documentation, yet digital tools are often unintuitive and repetitious while offering limited safety features. Modern perioperative care demands platforms that enhance clinical decision-making, improve efficiency, and promote evidence-based practice.

This is Periscope: an open-source perioperative care platform with a sophisticated safety algorithm that automatically detects high-risk features and suggests evidence-based interventions, referenced to society guidelines. The interface is rich with efficiency-focused tools and automations. Periscope automatically identifies candidates for audits and clinical trials, then enables single-click enrolment via integration with REDCap.
