The Problem

Perioperative care needs better tools.

Doctors and patients spend too much time repeating themselves and too little time getting people safely through their surgery.

Periscope is a better way.

Meet Periscope

Periscope has intelligent tools that take away the busiwork of perioperative care.

The interface is packed with features that make it easy to provide safe, efficient, and evidence-based care.

Free and Open

Periscope is free and open-source.

We think the best way to serve our mission is to make great tools available to everyone.

Please join us on GitHub and help us make Periscope the best it can be.
